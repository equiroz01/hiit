# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Pulse HIIT ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

Pulse HIIT stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Workout preferences:** Your custom timer settings (work time, rest time, rounds)
- **Workout history:** Records of completed workouts including date, duration, and rounds completed
- **App settings:** Language preference and favorite presets

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do **not** collect location data
- We do **not** collect health or fitness data beyond basic workout completion records
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **not** transmit any data to external servers

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data:

- Remains on your device only
- Is not synchronized to any cloud service
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

Pulse HIIT does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
