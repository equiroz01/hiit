# About Pulse HIIT

## What is Pulse HIIT?

Pulse HIIT is a simple, elegant interval timer designed for High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workouts. Whether you're doing Tabata, EMOM, or custom interval training, Pulse HIIT helps you stay focused on your workout without worrying about the clock.

## Key Features

### Customizable Timer
- Set your **work** interval (5 seconds to 5 minutes)
- Set your **rest** interval (5 seconds to 5 minutes)
- Choose the number of **rounds** (1 to 50)
- See the **total workout time** before starting

### Built-in Presets
Quick-start your workout with popular training protocols:

| Preset | Work | Rest | Rounds | Total Time |
|--------|------|------|--------|------------|
| **HIIT 30/30** | 30s | 30s | 10 | 10 min |
| **Tabata** | 20s | 10s | 8 | 4 min |
| **EMOM** | 40s | 20s | 10 | 10 min |
| **Cardio 45/15** | 45s | 15s | 12 | 12 min |
| **Intense 20/10** | 20s | 10s | 15 | 7.5 min |

### Clear Visual Feedback
- Large, easy-to-read timer display
- Color-coded phases: **green for work**, **blue for rest**
- Progress dots showing completed rounds
- Motivational phrases to keep you going

### Haptic Feedback
- Feel vibrations when phases change
- Countdown alerts in the last 3 seconds
- Triple vibration when workout is complete

### Workout Statistics
- Track your **daily streak**
- View **weekly workout count**
- See **total work minutes**
- Review your **recent workouts**

## How to Use

### Starting a Workout

1. **Choose a preset** from the quick workout section, OR
2. **Customize your intervals** using the +/- buttons for work, rest, and rounds
3. Tap **START**
4. Get ready during the 5-second preparation countdown
5. Follow the timer through work and rest phases
6. Celebrate when you finish!

### During a Workout

- **PAUSE** - Stop the timer temporarily
- **RESUME** - Continue from where you paused
- **Reset** - Start the workout over from the beginning
- **Finish** - End the workout early (partial progress is saved)

### Managing Presets

- Tap **See all** to view all available presets
- Tap the **star** to add/remove from favorites
- **Long press** to delete custom presets

## What is HIIT?

**High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)** alternates between intense exercise bursts and rest periods. Benefits include:

- Burns more calories in less time
- Improves cardiovascular fitness
- Boosts metabolism
- No equipment required
- Workouts as short as 4 minutes

## Privacy

Pulse HIIT respects your privacy:
- All data stays on your device
- No accounts or sign-ups required
- No ads or tracking
- Works completely offline
