# Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is Pulse HIIT free to use?**
A: Yes, Pulse HIIT is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**
A: Yes, Pulse HIIT works entirely offline. No internet connection is required.

**Q: What devices are supported?**
A: Pulse HIIT is available for iOS, Android, and web browsers.

---

### Timer & Workouts

**Q: How do I start a workout?**
A: From the home screen, select a preset or customize your work/rest times and rounds, then tap "START".

**Q: Can I pause during a workout?**
A: Yes, tap "PAUSE" at any time. Tap "RESUME" to continue or "Finish" to end early.

**Q: What is the preparation countdown?**
A: Before each workout begins, you get 5 seconds to get ready. This helps you prepare for the first work interval.

**Q: Why does my screen stay on during workouts?**
A: The app keeps your screen active so you can always see the timer without unlocking your device.

---

### Presets

**Q: How do I create a custom preset?**
A: Currently, you can customize your workout settings directly on the home screen. The app remembers your last configuration.

**Q: How do I mark a preset as favorite?**
A: Go to "See all" presets and tap the star icon next to any preset to add it to your favorites.

**Q: Can I delete presets?**
A: You can delete custom presets by long-pressing on them. Default presets cannot be deleted.

---

### Statistics

**Q: How is my streak calculated?**
A: Your streak counts consecutive days with at least one completed workout.

**Q: Why did my streak reset?**
A: If you miss a day without completing a workout, your streak resets to zero.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally on your device. It is not synced to the cloud.

---

### Settings

**Q: How do I change the language?**
A: Go to Settings (gear icon) and select your preferred language from the list.

**Q: What languages are available?**
A: English, Spanish, Italian, French, and Chinese.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Timer not working properly

1. Close and reopen the app
2. Make sure your device is not in low power mode
3. Check that notifications are enabled for sound alerts

### Data lost after update

Your workout history is stored locally. If data was lost:
1. This may occur if you reinstalled the app
2. Unfortunately, local data cannot be recovered once deleted

### App crashes

1. Update to the latest version of the app
2. Restart your device
3. Reinstall the app if the problem persists
